Help - Bitcoin Stats

Overview

This widget provides real-time Bitcoin statistics, updated every 60 seconds using CoinGecko and Mempool APIs. It features a dark/light theme toggle and clickable stats that link to additional resources.

Key Stats Explained

  • Sats per $1: Number of satoshis (smallest Bitcoin unit) per USD. Shows current value (pulsing orange) and all-time low (green). Click to open USD to Sats/BTC Converter.
  • BTC Price: Current Bitcoin price in USD (pulsing orange) and all-time high (green). Click for CoinMarketCap Bitcoin page.
  • Price Changes: Percentage changes over 24h, 6 months, and 1 year (green for positive, red for negative). Click to open detailed Price Change page with more timeframes (24h to all-time).
  • Block Info: Current block number, hash rate (EH/s), and estimated next halving date. Click for Mempool.space explorer.
  • BTC Dominance & Fear/Greed: Bitcoin's market dominance (%) and Fear & Greed Index (red for fear, green for greed, yellow for neutral). Click for Bitcoin White Paper (Optimized for seamless mobile viewing).
  • Market Cap & Volume: Bitcoin market cap (formatted as $T/B/M), 24h trading volume, and circulating supply out of 21M. Click for Bitcoin Wikipedia page.

Interactive Features

  • Clickable Stats: Tap/click any stat box to open related pages or external links (opens in new tab).
  • Theme Toggle: Moon/Sun icon (bottom-left) switches between dark and light modes. Preference saved locally.
  • Help Button: Question mark icon (bottom-right) opens this help page.

Data Sources & Notes

  • Data from CoinGecko (prices, market data) and Mempool (block info, hash rate).
  • All-time low Sats per $1 tracked locally in browser storage.
  • Updates every 60 seconds; "Loading..." or "Error" if fetch fails.
  • Responsive design for mobile/desktop; hover effects on desktop.
